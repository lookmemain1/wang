1. Purpose and how to use this document
This document provides five synthetic Indonesian clinical documentation samples and the structural rules they follow. It is intended as the input set for the Path 1 demo build (3-week scripted-tablet demo) and as the FHIR-mapping reference for the Path 2 white-label integration.
The samples are not real patient records. They are constructed from public Indonesian regulation and clinical guidelines, then reviewed against typical content of published Indonesian case reports. There is no PDP Law exposure and no hospital approval required for use.
Source basis
•	Permenkes No. 24 Tahun 2022 (Rekam Medis Elektronik) — minimum data elements for outpatient, inpatient, and discharge records.
•	Permenkes No. 269 Tahun 2008 (Rekam Medis) — legacy structure still referenced in hospital SOPs.
•	SatuSehat / Indonesia Health Services (IHS) FHIR R4 Profiles (Kemenkes, 2023–2025) — official JSON schemas for Patient, Encounter, Condition, Observation, Procedure, MedicationRequest, Composition.
•	KARS Standar Nasional Akreditasi Rumah Sakit (SNARS) — documentation format requirements for accreditation.
•	Formularium Nasional (Fornas) 2024 — BPJS-mandated drug list and INN naming.
•	PERSI / IDI clinical practice guidelines for the conditions referenced (T2DM-HTN, DHF, TB, geriatric polypharmacy).
•	Style and case mix benchmarked against published Indonesian case reports in eJKI, MKB, JIK, and CDK (without reproducing any single article).
How engineers should use these templates
1.	Treat each Section (3–7) as a self-contained clinical document. The narrative text is what the doctor speaks or types; the structured fields are what the AI must extract; the FHIR mapping shows what gets transmitted to SatuSehat.
2.	Bahasa Indonesia is the primary narrative language. English and Latin medical terms appear naturally within Bahasa sentences (code-switching). The ambient-AI demo must handle mixed-language transcription.
3.	All diagnoses are coded with ICD-10. All procedures are coded with ICD-9-CM. Both are required for INA-CBGs / BPJS claim submission.
4.	All medications use Fornas INN names by default. Brand-name aliases are listed in Appendix B for the drug-interaction alert demo.
5.	De-identified placeholders are used throughout: [NAMA PASIEN], [NIK], [NO. REKAM MEDIS], [NAMA DOKTER], [NAMA FASKES]. The build can substitute these at runtime.
Caveat: real-world clinical documents vary by SIM RS vendor (eClaim, Khanza, SIMRS GOS, custom systems). These templates reflect the regulatory floor every facility must meet, not any specific vendor's format. A short clinician review (Hafidz, Dr. Agnes, or Bu Nin — 1 hour each) is recommended before final freeze.
 
2. Permenkes 24/2022 minimum fields and SatuSehat FHIR mapping
The table below lists the fields every Indonesian medical record must contain per Permenkes 24/2022, with their canonical SatuSehat FHIR resource mapping. Use this as the schema reference for the AI's extraction layer.
Clinical field (Bahasa / English)	SatuSehat FHIR resource and path
Identitas pasien (NIK, nama, tanggal lahir, jenis kelamin)	Patient.identifier, Patient.name, Patient.birthDate, Patient.gender
Tanggal & waktu pelayanan	Encounter.period.start / .end
Jenis pelayanan (rawat jalan / rawat inap / IGD)	Encounter.class (AMB / IMP / EMER)
Anamnesis (riwayat penyakit sekarang, dahulu, keluarga)	Encounter.reasonCode + Observation (clinical history LOINC 11329-0)
Tanda vital (TD, HR, RR, SpO2, suhu)	Observation (LOINC 85354-9 BP panel, 8867-4 HR, 9279-1 RR, 2708-6 SpO2, 8310-5 temp)
Pemeriksaan fisik	Observation (multiple, body-site-specific)
Pemeriksaan penunjang (lab, radiologi)	Observation, DiagnosticReport, ImagingStudy
Diagnosis (kerja / banding / akhir) dengan kode ICD-10	Condition.code (ICD-10), Condition.clinicalStatus, Condition.verificationStatus
Tindakan medis dengan kode ICD-9-CM	Procedure.code (ICD-9-CM), Procedure.performedDateTime
Terapi / pengobatan (resep)	MedicationRequest.medicationCodeableConcept (Fornas / KFA code), .dosageInstruction
Edukasi & rencana lanjutan	CarePlan.activity, CarePlan.note
DPJP & tanda tangan	Encounter.participant (PPJP / DPJP), Practitioner.identifier (SIP)
Resume medis pulang	Composition (LOINC 18842-5 Discharge summary)

•	Case: Kunjungan kontrol Prolanis — T2DM + Hipertensi pada pasien dewasa.

•	Document format: SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) — universal in Indonesia.

3.5  Demo angle for this template
This case demonstrates all three AI features in a single common scenario:
•	Ambient note-taking: doctor narrates in Bahasa + English; AI produces SOAP-structured note with vitals auto-extracted.
•	Insurance coding: AI suggests E11.9 + I10 (primary + secondary), flags candidate upgrade to E11.21 if UACR confirmed.
•	Drug interaction alert: Captopril + Aspirin (mild risk: reduced ACE-I efficacy and renal vasoconstriction in dehydration); Glimepiride hypoglycaemia caution in elderly; Simvastatin + Amlodipine (limit Simvastatin to 20mg per FDA advisory).
 
4. Template 2 — Catatan Rawat Inap (Inpatient Admission & Progress Note)
Setting and case profile
•	Setting: Hospital, Internal Medicine ward (Class C or B).
•	Case: Dengue Hemorrhagic Fever (DHF / DBD), grade II.
•	Why this case: Endemic, high admission frequency, demonstrates inpatient progression (admission → daily progress → plasma leakage monitoring).
•	Document format: Lembar masuk (admission), CPPT (Catatan Perkembangan Pasien Terintegrasi), discharge plan.
4.1  Lembar masuk (Admission Note)
Tanggal/Jam MRS  : [DD-MM-YYYY] / [HH:MM] WIB
Cara masuk       : Rujukan dari Klinik Pratama [NAMA] dengan suspek DBD
Ruang rawat      : [RUANG]  Kelas: 1 / 2 / 3 / VIP
Penjamin         : BPJS Kesehatan (PBI / Non-PBI) / Asuransi / Pribadi
DPJP             : dr. [NAMA DOKTER], Sp.PD
PPJP             : Ns. [NAMA PERAWAT]
Anamnesis
Pasien perempuan, usia 24 tahun, datang dengan keluhan demam tinggi sejak 4 hari sebelum masuk rumah sakit (4 HSMRS). Demam mendadak tinggi, terus-menerus, sempat turun dengan parasetamol tapi naik lagi. Disertai sakit kepala, nyeri belakang mata (retro-orbital pain), nyeri otot dan sendi (myalgia + arthralgia). Sejak 1 HSMRS, demam mulai turun tapi muncul mimisan ringan dan gusi sedikit berdarah saat sikat gigi. Mual (+), muntah 2x isi cairan dan makanan, tidak ada darah. BAB normal, tidak hitam. BAK pekat, frekuensi berkurang. Tidak ada batuk pilek.
Riwayat lingkungan: tetangga sebelah rumah dirawat karena DBD minggu lalu. Pasien tinggal di daerah dengan kasus dengue endemik (Jakarta Selatan).
Riwayat penyakit dahulu: tidak ada. Tidak ada alergi obat. Tidak ada riwayat penyakit kronis.
Pemeriksaan fisik saat masuk
Keadaan umum    : tampak sakit sedang, compos mentis, GCS 15 (E4M6V5)
TD              : 100/70 mmHg          MAP: 80
HR              : 102 x/menit, reguler, isi cukup
RR              : 22 x/menit
Suhu            : 37.8 °C (sudah turun dari 39.5 °C pagi tadi)
SpO2            : 99% room air
BB              : 52 kg
Kulit           : petechiae (+) ekstremitas atas dan bawah; rumple-leede (+) >20 spot/inch²
Kepala          : conjunctiva tidak anemis, sklera tidak ikterik, mukosa bibir kering
Leher           : KGB tidak membesar
Thorax          : Cor S1 S2 reguler, murmur (-). Pulmo vesikuler, ronchi (-/-)
Abdomen         : supel, nyeri tekan epigastrium (+), hepatomegali (+) 2 cm BAC, lien tidak teraba
Ekstremitas     : akral dingin, CRT 3 detik, edema (-/-)
Hasil laboratorium saat masuk
Hematologi lengkap:
  Hb            : 14.8 g/dL          (baseline pasien tidak diketahui)
  Ht            : 46 %               (>20% increase dari baseline normal ~38% → plasma leakage)
  Leukosit      : 3,200 /μL          (leukopenia)
  Trombosit     : 68,000 /μL         (trombositopenia berat)
  Eritrosit     : 5.1 juta/μL
  Differential  : Neut 38%, Limf 48%, Mono 10%, Eos 2%, Baso 2% (limfositosis relatif)

Kimia darah:
  SGOT/SGPT     : 86 / 102 U/L       (hepatitis ringan, khas dengue)
  Ureum/Creat   : 22 / 0.8 mg/dL     (dbn)
  Elektrolit    : Na 134, K 3.6, Cl 100 mEq/L

Serologi:
  NS1 antigen   : Positif (++)
  IgM dengue    : Positif
  IgG dengue    : Positif (infeksi sekunder)
Assessment dan plan saat masuk
Diagnosis kerja:
1. Dengue Hemorrhagic Fever (DHF) grade II
   ICD-10: A91 (Dengue haemorrhagic fever)
   Justifikasi: demam + trombositopenia <100K + Ht meningkat + manifestasi perdarahan (petechiae, mimisan, gusi berdarah)
   Fase: critical phase (defervescence day, hari ke-5)

Diagnosis banding:
   - Dengue Fever (DF) — disingkirkan karena ada plasma leakage + bleeding
   - Demam tifoid — disingkirkan klinis

Plan:
1. Rawat inap di bangsal penyakit dalam, isolasi standar
2. Tirah baring, kepala lebih tinggi
3. Pantau vital sign tiap 4 jam, tanda syok (cold extremities, narrow pulse pressure <20 mmHg)
4. IVFD Ring Lactate (RL) 5 ml/kgBB/jam = 260 ml/jam (sesuaikan responsivitas)
5. Cek Hb, Ht, trombosit tiap 12 jam (lebih sering jika syok mengancam)
6. Diet TKTP cair-lunak, banyak minum oralit
7. Tidak diberikan: NSAID, aspirin, kortikosteroid
8. Paracetamol 500 mg PRN (max 4x/hari) jika demam >38.5 °C
9. Edukasi keluarga: tanda warning (vomiting persisten, sakit perut hebat, perdarahan masif, gelisah → lapor segera)

S: Demam masih ada tapi turun, terasa lemas, mual berkurang. BAK lebih sering
   sejak diinfus. Tidak ada perdarahan baru.

   Hb 14.2, Ht 44, Leuko 3,800, Trombosit 52,000 (turun lagi)

   Hemodinamik stabil, plasma leakage terkontrol dengan IVFD, trombosit
   masih turun (expected nadir hari ke-6-7).
P: Lanjut IVFD RL 3 ml/kgBB/jam, evaluasi balance cairan tiap 4 jam.
   Cek Hb/Ht/trombosit ulang sore.

Verifikasi DPJP: dr. [NAMA], Sp.PD  [TTD/elektronik]

4.4  Demo angle
•	Ambient note-taking: long anamnesis with multiple symptoms must be parsed into discrete fields (febrile days, warning signs, bleeding manifestations).
•	Insurance coding: A91 maps cleanly to INA-CBG group; AI must auto-distinguish A90 (Dengue Fever) vs A91 (DHF) based on platelet + hematocrit thresholds.
•	Drug interaction alert: hard contraindication on NSAIDs (Ibuprofen, Diclofenac) and Aspirin during active DHF — the AI must block these even if a junior doctor prescribes for fever or pain.
